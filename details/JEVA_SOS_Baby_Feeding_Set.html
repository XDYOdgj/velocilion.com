JEVA SOS Baby Feeding Set

A smart and secure choice for parents looking to introduce their little ones to self-feeding. This comprehensive baby feeding set is designed with your baby's comfort and safety in mind. Made from premium, food-grade silicone, it's free from harmful BPA, ensuring peace of mind with every bite.

The strong suction base helps keep meals mess-free, making it easier for your baby to explore food while staying tidy. The ergonomic design of the utensils allows little hands to grip and use them with ease, supporting a smooth transition into independent eating. This baby led weaning set includes everything you need: a divided suction plate, suction bowl, adjustable bib, drinking cup, and a bonus teether to help with teething discomfort.

All parts are easy to clean, dishwasher safe, and reusable in microwave, fridge, and freezer. Whether it's a birthday, baby shower, or a special milestone celebration, this set makes an ideal gift for your little one.
