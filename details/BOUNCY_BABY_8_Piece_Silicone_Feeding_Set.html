8-Piece Silicone Feeding Set for Babies

Designed for the first stage of feeding, this versatile 8-piece set includes plates, bowls, a cup, straw, fork, spoon, bib, and snack cup. It's the perfect tool to help your baby explore food and develop mealtime independence with ease.

Crafted from 100% food-grade silicone, this set is safe, durable, and easy to clean. The soft, flexible material ensures your baby's comfort and confidence during feeding, making mealtimes more enjoyable for both.

Each piece features secure suction technology that keeps everything in place, reducing spills and promoting a mess-free feeding experience. The ergonomic design supports your baby's natural grip, helping them learn to self-feed with greater control.

Available in a range of vibrant colors, this feeding set is not only functional but also a delightful addition to your baby's meal routine. Choose from 5 color options to match your style and keep your little one engaged.
