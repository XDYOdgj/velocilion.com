Safe & Fun Learning Tools for Toddlers

These 3-piece baby utensils are designed to make mealtime both safe and enjoyable for little ones. Perfect for toddlers starting to explore feeding on their own, they include a spoon, fork, and cup that are ideal for babies aged 6 to 12 months. Made from food-grade, BPA-free silicone, they are free from harmful substances like lead and phthalates, ensuring a non-toxic experience for your growing child.

The ergonomic handles and weighted cups provide excellent grip, making it easier for small hands to hold and use. This thoughtful design supports self-feeding and promotes independence during baby-led weaning. Each utensil is carefully crafted to maintain a safe distance between the fork and baffle, preventing the risk of swallowing small parts and giving parents peace of mind.
